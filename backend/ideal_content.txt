Solution Approach

RPA Solution Approach

The below AutomationEdge robotic process automation (RPA) and Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) solution offers enhanced accuracy, efficiency and time-saving advantages for your business processes.

RPA is ideal when processes involve structured data, or when automating interactions with systems that don't provide API integration, enhancing efficiency without compromising security.

The platform offers multiple recorders that capture and replicate human actions, making it easy to automate processes by mimicking the exact steps performed by a user. When security or system limitations restrict API exposure, this makes it a potent tool that enables the rapid and precise production of bots without requiring a lot of programming.

When legacy systems cannot be upgraded or directly integrated with newer platforms, RPA acts as a bridge by replicating human interactions with these systems, ensuring data transfer and task completion without needing extensive reprogramming or system changes.

The platform can also read and write data using SQL queries on popular relational databases such as Oracle, SQL Server, and MS Access, making it highly versatile for database management.

AutomationEdge bots are equipped to send email alerts, notifications, and generate dashboards or reports to keep stakeholders informed of the automation process. This visibility helps monitor and manage automated workflows effectively.
